A well-functioning digestive system is central to overall health, nutrient absorption, and immune resilience. Chronic digestive complaints—such as bloating, acid reflux, and irregular bowel habits—are often amplified by modern lifestyles and chronic stress. We approach digestive disorders by restoring the core functional energy of the digestive organs and re-establishing harmony throughout the gastrointestinal tract.

## 1. Traditional Insights into Digestive Disorders

- **Digestive Vitality (Spleen/Stomach Qi):** When core digestive energy is weakened by fatigue or irregular habits, the body struggles to process food efficiently, leading to bloating and low stamina.

- **Brain-Gut Connection (Liver Qi):** Emotional stress can cause energy congestion that "invades" the digestive system, manifesting as stress-induced cramps or acid reflux.

- **Metabolic Stasis:** An accumulation of internal metabolic waste can disrupt gut health, leading to chronic inflammation and altered elimination.
