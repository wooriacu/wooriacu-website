Diabetes is a chronic metabolic condition that requires consistent monitoring and medical management. From a Traditional Korean Medicine (TKM) perspective, we view this as a systemic imbalance—historically described as *Sogal-jeung* (wasting and thirsting)—where the body's essential fluids and metabolic vitality are depleted. Our approach focuses on supporting your body's natural regulatory systems alongside your standard clinical care.

## 1. Traditional and Modern Insights

- **Systemic Imbalance:** We look beyond blood glucose metrics to understand how chronic stress, digestive fatigue, and internal heat (Fire) affect your body's ability to maintain homeostasis.

- **Recognizing Signals:** Symptoms such as frequent thirst, fatigue, or numbness are often the body's way of signaling metabolic stress. Recognizing these signs early allows for more proactive, comprehensive self-care.

## 2. Complementary TKM Approaches at Woori Acupuncture

- **Constitutional Herbal Support:** We provide customized formulas designed to replenish essential moisture and support the body's internal environment, helping you maintain a more stable metabolic foundation.

- **Acupuncture for Circulation:** By stimulating specific meridians, we help improve microcirculation and support the nervous system, assisting your body in its natural regulatory efforts.

## 3. Essential Daily Management

- **Dietary and Physical Balance:** Consistent activity, such as lower-body movement after meals, is vital. We encourage a mindful approach to nutrition that respects your unique digestive capacity.

- **Stress Modulation:** Prioritizing quality sleep and relaxation helps maintain autonomic balance, which is crucial for overall metabolic stability.

**Important Note:** Diabetes management requires a coordinated approach. Please continue all prescribed treatments and monitoring as directed by your primary physician. Our supportive clinical care is designed to be integrated into your existing regimen.
