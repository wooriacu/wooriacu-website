## Neck, Back & Joints

Chronic spinal discomfort and joint stiffness are rarely just isolated mechanical issues. In traditional Eastern medicine, these conditions are often associated with structural imbalances combined with localized stagnation (traditionally viewed as *Cold-Damp* or *Blood Stasis*). When foundational body reserves diminish due to overwork or aging, joint matrices can lose their natural lubrication, leading to friction and restricted movement. Our approach focuses on easing this internal congestion, promoting healthy microcirculation around the spine, and supporting the surrounding tissues to encourage smoother and more comfortable mobility.

**Conditions We Care For:**

- Herniated Discs (Cervical & Lumbar)

- Spinal Stenosis

- Chronic Low Back Pain

- Frozen Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Issues

- Knee Osteoarthritis

## Muscles & Injuries

Whether resulting from sports injuries, repetitive strain, or chronic poor posture, soft tissue discomfort often triggers defensive muscular bracing. This persistent localized tension can lead to a state of *Blood Stasis*, where restricted microcirculation limits the smooth flow of fresh nutrients to the fibers, slowing down the body's natural healing process. By targeting affected meridians and neuromuscular motor points, our clinical care aims to ease deep muscle spasms, lessen fascial restrictions, and encourage regional blood flow to support the body's baseline recovery and soft tissue resilience.

**Conditions We Care For:**

- Acute Muscle Spasms (Catching a cold in the muscle)

- Ankle, Wrist & Knee Sprains

- Myofascial Pain Syndrome (MPS)

- Tennis & Golfer's Elbow

- Tendinitis & Plantar Fasciitis

## Nerve Pain & Numbness

Radiating discomfort, sharp sensations, and chronic numbness are often closely associated with localized structural tension and restricted circulation within the deep tissue pathways. In Eastern physiology, these symptoms are understood as a state of deep obstruction within the collateral channels, where a lack of smooth blood flow leaves the local pathways vulnerable to hypersensitivity or altered sensation. Our clinical care focuses on soothing regional tension, promoting healthy microcirculation throughout the pathways, and supporting the body in gently easing these uncomfortable, lingering sensations.

**Conditions We Care For:**

- Sciatica (Radiating leg pain)

- Carpal Tunnel Syndrome

- Peripheral Neuropathy (Numbness in hands & feet)

- Facial Palsy (Bell's Palsy)

- Post-Herpetic Neuralgia

## Headaches & Chronic Pain

Chronic headaches and pain of unidentifiable origin are closely linked to persistent stress and accumulated systemic fatigue. When the body remains in a prolonged state of tension or hyperarousal, it can lead to what traditional Eastern medicine recognizes as pathological internal heat, which may increase sensory sensitivity to discomfort. Our clinical care focuses on easing physical tension, addressing circulatory imbalances, and supporting the enhancement of the body's natural resilience to help manage chronic physical discomfort.

**Conditions We Care For:**

- Tension Headaches & Migraines

- Fibromyalgia (Widespread chronic pain)

- Stress-Induced Chronic Body Aches

- Chronic Fatigue Syndrome with Pain
