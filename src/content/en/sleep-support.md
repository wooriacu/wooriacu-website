Quality sleep is the foundation of overall vitality, immune resilience, and cognitive clarity. In traditional Eastern medicine, we understand sleep disturbances not merely as nighttime restlessness, but as a systemic reflection of an overactive nervous system or underlying physical exhaustion that prevents the body from transitioning into deep, restorative rest. Our clinical approach focuses on restoring this natural equilibrium to help you reclaim your sleep health.

## 1. Traditional Insights into Sleep Disturbances

- **Hyperactivity of Heart-Fire:** Chronic stress or overthinking can cause an accumulation of internal heat, keeping the mind hypervigilant and the nervous system overstimulated.

- **Energetic Disharmony:** Healthy sleep relies on the balanced communication between the upper and lower body's energy systems. When this "water-fire" axis is disrupted, the mind remains restless, leading to frequent waking or fragmented rest.

- **Physical Depletion:** Long-term exhaustion or nutritional insufficiency can leave the nervous system without the necessary resources to "anchor" the spirit at night, causing early morning waking or light, dream-disturbed sleep.

## 2. Clinical Modalities at Woori Acupuncture

- **Constitutional Herbal Support:** We provide customized herbal formulations designed to gently soothe an overactive nervous system, clear internal heat, and nourish the body's internal reserves, helping you move toward a natural, unassisted sleep cycle.

- **Autonomic Regulation via Acupuncture:** Acupuncture is exceptionally effective at shifting the nervous system from a stressed "fight-or-flight" state into a calm, parasympathetic "rest-and-digest" mode. By relaxing muscular tension and balancing meridians, we prepare the mind and body for deep recovery.
