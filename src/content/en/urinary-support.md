Our supportive care focuses on restoring internal warmth, lifting diminished core energy, and promoting healthy circulation to foster a more stable pelvic environment.

## 1. Traditional Insights into Pelvic Stability

- **Declining Core Warmth (Kidney Qi):** When foundational warmth diminishes—due to aging or physical exhaustion—the bladder loses its necessary energetic support, which may manifest as urgency or weakened control.

- **Downward Shift of Vital Energy:** Prolonged overexertion can cause the body's central supportive energy to weaken. Without this natural "lifting" force, the lower abdomen may feel heavy, and bladder resilience can diminish under physical pressure.

- **Circulatory Stagnation:** Internal imbalances can lead to localized congestion, which may cause bladder sensitivity or frequent disruptions.
