Achieving and maintaining a healthy weight is fundamentally connected to metabolic vitality, hormonal harmony, and systemic cellular health. In Traditional Korean Medicine, we view weight management not as a simple caloric equation, but as an optimization of your metabolic foundation. Our clinical approach focuses on strengthening your digestive vitality, clearing internal stagnation, and fostering systemic resilience to help you regain your natural metabolic efficiency.

## 1. Traditional Insights into Metabolic Health

- **Spleen Vitality & Metabolism:** When the Spleen system—responsible for transforming food into energy—is weakened, the body fails to process nutrients effectively. Instead of being used as vitality, these resources settle as fluid retention and lethargy.

- **Metabolic Accumulation (Phlegm-Dampness):** Internal sluggishness can lead to a state known as Phlegm-Dampness. This metabolic waste creates internal congestion, slowing down energy expenditure and making it difficult for the body to maintain its natural rhythm.

- **Stomach-Heat & Appetite:** Chronic stress can generate internal heat, which often mimics false hunger signals, leading to carbohydrate cravings and digestive irritation.
