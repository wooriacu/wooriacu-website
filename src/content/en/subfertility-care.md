Eastern medicine traditionally compares a healthy uterine and ovarian environment to 'sowing a healthy seed into fertile, welcoming soil.' - **Ample Nourishment and Moisture (Blood and Fluids):** For soil to remain fertile and resilient, it requires continuous enrichment. Adequate, unobstructed circulation to the lower abdomen provides the essential nutrients needed to support a plush, welcoming, and grounded endometrial baseline.

- **Warm Yang Energy (陽氣):** The driving force of life activities is sustained by *Yang* energy, which provides essential physical warmth.
