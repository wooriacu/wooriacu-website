**Beyond Form, Managing 'Qi':** Acupuncture is not merely a physical act of stimulating muscles or bones. It is a sophisticated therapeutic method that regulates and modulates the movement of 'Qi,' the invisible energy within the body, through the pathways of the physical form.

**A Precise Energy Map: 12 Meridians and the Extraordinary Eight Vessels:** Our bodies possess 12 precise highways through which Qi flows (the 12 meridians) and fundamental reservoirs (the Extraordinary Eight Vessels) that store and supply energy during times of need. Acupuncture follows this intricate energy map to locate blockages and open the pathways for Qi to flow.

**Opening the Gateway for Flow:** Much like opening the entrance of a water channel when a field is cracked by drought, allowing water to flow freely again, acupuncture serves as a tool to gently open the gateways where Qi flows in our bodies. By clearing these water channels through acupuncture, the internal Qi circulates freely once more, allowing stiffened muscles and tissues to regain the strength to heal themselves.

**Restoring Fundamental Homeostasis:** By regulating the movement of Qi—which forms the *Qi-Mi-Hyeong-Saek* (energy, taste, form, and color)—the body naturally restores its original, healthy balance. This approach does not fixate on physical form but instead addresses the deficiency or stagnation of Qi to treat the root cause.
