# Workers' Compensation Acupuncture Care – Woori Acupuncture

## MTUS-Compliant Care for Workplace Injuries

At Woori Acupuncture, we specialize in acupuncture treatments for work-related injuries in Riverside. Our goal is to help patients reduce pain, improve mobility, and return to work safely and efficiently.

## Why Choose Us?

- **MTUS-Compliant Treatment Plans** – All therapies follow California Workers' Compensation guidelines.

- **Functional Improvement Focused** – We track progress in sitting, lifting, walking, and daily activity tolerance.

- **Fast Reporting & Authorization** – RFA and progress reports submitted within 24–48 hours to ensure smooth approval.

- **Experienced with MedRisk & OneCall** – Trusted provider for workers' compensation networks.

## How It Works

1. **Referral or Walk-In** – We accept patients referred by PTPs or Workers' Compensation networks.

2. **Personalized Treatment Plan** – MTUS-based acupuncture tailored to each patient's injury and functional goals.

3. **Progress Tracking & Reporting** – Regular updates and reports submitted promptly to your employer, PTP, or insurance network.

4. **Return to Work** – Our ultimate goal is to restore function and work capacity safely and efficiently.
