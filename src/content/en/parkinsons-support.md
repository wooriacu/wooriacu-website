Parkinson's disease is a chronic neurodegenerative condition that requires careful, long-term management. Modern medicine provides essential symptomatic relief, such as dopamine replacement, which is vital for maintaining daily motor function. At Woori Acupuncture, we provide integrative, constitutional care designed to support your foundational health, working in harmony with your current medical regimen.

## 1. A Holistic Perspective on Parkinson's Care

We view Parkinson's disease as an accumulation of systemic imbalances that affect the body's internal environment. While modern medicine addresses the deficiency of dopamine, we focus on the "Root"—the body's constitutional state—to ensure that the physical "Branch" (motor symptoms) can be managed more sustainably. By improving your overall energy (Qi) and blood circulation, we aim to support your body's self-regulatory capacity.

## 2. Integrating Dopamine and Body-Mind Harmony

In Korean medicine, the body is an organic integration of structure (Hyeong) and functional energy (Gi).

- **The Power of Gi-bun:** Neurotransmitters like dopamine function best when the body is in a state of internal balance. By addressing emotional stagnation (Chil-jeong-sang) and restoring the harmony of your internal organs, we help create an environment where your nervous system can function more efficiently.

- **Addressing Early Signals:** We pay close attention to premonitory signs, such as sleep disturbances, chronic constipation, or changes in sensory perception. Addressing these systemic imbalances early may help support your overall quality of life.

## 3. Integrative Clinical Modalities

- **Customized Herbal Support:** We formulate herbal prescriptions tailored to your unique constitution. These are designed to harmonize the Heart and Kidney systems, improve cerebral circulation, and mitigate the physical tension associated with chronic stress and stagnation.

- **Acupuncture for Neuromuscular Stability:** Targeted acupuncture helps alleviate muscle rigidity and tremors by balancing the autonomic nervous system and promoting smooth circulation of Qi and blood.

**Important Note on Coordinated Care:** Parkinson's disease management requires a coordinated approach. **Please do not modify or discontinue your prescribed dopamine medications without consulting your neurologist.** Our supportive care is intended to complement, not replace, your existing medical regimen. We work in tandem with your current treatments to support your resilience and overall well-being.
