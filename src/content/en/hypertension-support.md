Hypertension is a chronic condition characterized by persistently elevated blood pressure. From a Traditional Korean Medicine (TKM) perspective, we view this not merely as an isolated metric, but as a systemic imbalance in the harmony of Qi (vital energy) and blood circulation. Our goal is to support your vascular health and total body harmony alongside your standard medical care.

## 1. Contributing Factors and Internal Imbalances

- **Liver Yang Hyperactivity:** Stress and fatigue can generate internal "Fire," which rushes upward, contributing to pressure and tension.

- **Yin Deficiency:** As the body ages or becomes overworked, essential fluids (Yin) deplete, making it harder to regulate the body's warming energy (Yang).

- **Vascular Resistance:** The buildup of metabolic waste (Phlegm) and sluggish circulation (Blood Stasis) can increase resistance within the vessels, driving pressure upward.

## 2. Complementary TKM Approaches at Woori Acupuncture

- **Customized Herbal Support:** Tailored formulas are designed to cool internal heat, replenish essential fluids, and support healthy circulation, helping to purify the vascular environment from metabolic accumulation.

- **Acupuncture Treatment:** Targeted acupuncture promotes smooth circulation, helps alleviate tension in the blood vessels, and balances the autonomic nervous system to support stable vascular function.

## 3. Essential Daily Management

- **Balanced Lifestyle:** A low-sodium diet rich in potassium-dense foods, combined with consistent, moderate aerobic exercise, is foundational for vascular elasticity.

- **Stress Modulation:** Mindfulness, meditation, and deep breathing are essential for soothing the nervous system and maintaining stable pressure.

**Important Note:** Hypertension requires consistent monitoring and management. Please do not modify or discontinue any prescribed blood pressure medications without direct consultation with your primary physician. Our supportive care is intended to complement, not replace, your existing medical regimen.
